Our Journey

Founded by Yash Shah in 2021, EcoLattice grew out of a materials experiment at Central Saint Martins, London. What started as a university project quietly became something more. Today it's a UK and India based company working across automotive, interiors and wellness sectors, with Harinad P.M. leading product innovation and Pragya Agarwal driving materials and sustainability.

Founded 2021
Office UK & India

Our Values

Our values define how we work and inform every decision we make. Still forming, still evolving, but foundational to everything EcoLattice has built so far.

Sustainability

We are a team driven by curiosity and purpose, always pushing further and open to what comes next. We work across disciplines, geographies, and cultures, bringing together designers, engineers, and material thinkers to create conscious outcomes.

Design for Wellbeing

Every product we design puts the human first. Ergonomic thinking is built into our process from the start, ensuring the best possible comfort in every solution we make.

Learning And Curiosity

In a world that moves fast, we stay curious. We seek out new thinking, new perspectives, and new ways of working, applying what we learn every day to push our performance further and deliver outcomes that consistently exceed expectations.

Benchmarking

We test at every stage, no exceptions. Our materials, design choices, and processes are all put through rigorous validation to ensure that what we claim is what we deliver.

Global Reach

We work with a network of worldwide material partners that distribute and retail our products, bringing them closer to the designers and specifiers who matter the most. Through exhibitions and trade shows across the UK, US, Europe, and India, we have built relationships that go beyond transactions. Opening doors to new collaborations, new thinking, and a broader understanding of where design is heading.

Relevance

We have earned our place through consistency in delivering better solutions to pioneering companies. Our journey began from Green House - an incubation program at UAL to winning the IKEA Foam Accelerator, from showcases with Aram and Toyota Boshoku to awards, grants, and material library partnerships, our work has been recognised across industries and geographies.

Material & Design

EcoLattice balances performance, comfort and responsibility at every stage of the process. We work with global material libraries to stay at the frontier of what's possible, while partnering with today's visionary designers to bring fresh thinking and better outcomes to every product we make.
