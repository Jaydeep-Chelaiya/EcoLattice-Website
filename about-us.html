Who We Are

With a pioneering force in the foam industry, EcoLattice is dedicated to redefining material innovation with a commitment to sustainability. With our roots discovered back in 2020, we merged advanced technology with ecological responsibility to craft high-performance foam solutions.

Mission

We are transforming the foam industry with innovative, sustainable, and high-performance materials. Committed to advancing material science, we create eco-friendly solutions that enhance comfort and functionality while reducing environmental impact. Our mission is to lead the industry by example, promoting sustainability through precision and creativity.

Vision

We see a future where sustainable materials become standard across industries. As leaders in eco-friendly foam technology, our vision is to set new benchmarks for innovation and environmental responsibility, helping to create a healthier planet and better everyday experiences through our materials.

Value

Our values guide our commitment to excellence. Curiosity fuels our innovation, sustainability drives our decisions, and quality ensures our products excel. Collaboration with partners and clients leads to shared success, while integrity ensures we operate with transparency and accountability in everything we do.

Our Story

EcoLattice was established with the goal to change the foam products market by using environmentally friendly materials and innovative technologies. This journey was fueled by the vision to pursue sustainable solutions, providing high-performance customizable foams to cater to various industries ranging from ergonomic furniture to automotive or wellness industries.

EcoLattice was founded in 2020 as a university project focused on developing substitutes for foam and traditional production processes. This made people search for sustainable materials from waste which in turn boosted our mission. The project came into existence in 2023 when we started participating in design weeks internationally and cooperating with automotive, wellness, and luxury interior companies. In simple terms, our startup started in 2024.
