Why EcoLattice?

The details that set a new standard. Every characteristic is a deliberate engineering decision. Not a feature list, but a philosophy we embody.

Technology

Crafted through advanced manufacturing like additive manufacturing, binder jet technology. We use pressure mapping to deliver true customisation with an ergonomic, seamless structure that responds precisely where your body needs it.

Performance

EcoTPU™ is lightweight yet exceptionally durable, with an open-lattice structure that delivers natural breathability and is inherently water resistant, making it effortless to clean. Built to last, it stands up to both fire and UV exposure without degrading.

Sustainability

Made from recycled thermoplastic polyurethane, every piece is designed from the ground up with a considered material footprint with a cohesive structure that generates zero waste and is built for disassembly and reuse at end of life.
