About
Materials & Product

EcoLattice is changing sustainable materials by combining recycled thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) with 3D printing. Our foams offer a balance of flexibility, lightness, and performance. This allows us to deliver custom, high-performance solutions for automotive, interiors, and wellness. By transforming waste into circular resources, we are shaping the future of design and sustainability.

Our Innovation

EcoLattice advances the technology by creating foaming filaments and enhancing
the print parameters, software, and hardware. Hence, high-quality lattice foams are
achieved through our customised 3D printers with optimised print time.

Additive Manufacturing

Additive manufacturing allows us to create complex lattice structures quickly, accurately, and with minimal waste. This opens up designs that traditional foams cannot accomplish.

Design & Customisation

Customisation involves using software such as Grasshopper Rhino to convert pressure point data into designs by adjusting the foams' shore hardness and optimising the lattice structure.

Bio Materials

To ensure sustainable production, natural latex and algae composites are used alongside recycled thermoplastic elastomers. We are currently exploring bio-materials, and these are our upcoming products.
