Welcome to
EcoLattice

Transforming waste into circular resources, we are shaping the future of design and sustainability. EcoLattice offers sustainable materials by combining recycled thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) with 3D printing. Our foams offer a balance between flexibility and lightness to create customised, high-performance solutions for automotive, interiors, and wellness.

Why EcoLattice?

From zero waste to maximum durability, explore our modern and sustainable foam for your needs.

Sustainability

Crafted with eco-friendly materials, our products are designed to minimise environmental impact.

Customisation

Tailor-made foam solutions that fit perfectly into any design or application.

Varied Shore Hardness

EcoLattice developed foams with varied shore-hardness ranging the values of Shore A from 20 to 80. It is ideal for diverse applications, from interiors, automotive and healthcare use where customisation plays an integral part.

Lightweight & Breathability

Designed to maintain strength and comfort while allowing air circulation and providing fuel efficiency.

Water Resistant

Our foam resists moisture, ensuring long-lasting performance in wet conditions.

Easy To Clean

Designed for hassle-free maintenance, keeping your foam products looking new.

Durability

EcoLattice is built to withstand the test of time for 20 years, offering unmatched longevity and 4 times better than other foam.

Fire UV

Provides safety and protection, even under harsh conditions.

Recycled & Zero Waste

Foams are developed with recycled materials & zero-waste manufacturing process, guaranteeing that every product is created with a healthier environment in mind.

Our Innovation

EcoLattice advances the technology by creating foaming filaments and enhancing
the print parameters, software, and hardware. High-quality lattice foams are
achieved through our customised 3D printers with optimised print time.

Additive Manufacturing

Additive manufacturing allows us to create complex lattice structures quickly, accurately, and with minimal waste. This opens up designs that traditional foams cannot accomplish.

Design & Customisation

We use software such as Grasshopper Rhino to convert pressure point data into designs by adjusting the shore hardness and optimising the lattice structure.

Sustainable Materials

EcoLattice is designed for high-volume production and long-term circularity, ensuring a positive impact and overcoming the scale and consistency challenges faced by bio-based foams. It delivers enhanced mechanical and chemical properties while opening a world of design possibilities far beyond conventional foam blocks.
