Automotive

With materials that enhance comfort, safety, and sustainability, Ecolattice brings innovative foam solutions to the transportation and automotive sectors. Our advanced foam products are designed to meet the rigorous demands of automotive applications, offering superior durability, lightweight performance, and environmental responsibility.

Know More

Advantages

Ecolattice foam has several advantages, its main one being the durability for it has a 20-year lifespan and compression resistance. It is easy to wash and recycle hence making it sustainable, there are no complicated steps to follow. Its ability to be varied in shore hardness so that it can be made flexible for different uses while at the same time being very durable. With these features, Ecolattice foam is convenient and can be used in various ways.
