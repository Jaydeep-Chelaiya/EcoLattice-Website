About

The Contour reimagines the chair from the ground up - beginning not with convention, but with possibility. Designed by Harinad PM and produced by EcoLattice, it is fabricated entirely from recycled PETG using large-format additive manufacturing.

This approach eliminates the need for moulds, significantly reduces material waste, and enables production that responds directly to demand. The result is a continuous, fluid form where seat, back, and base resolve into a single gesture.
