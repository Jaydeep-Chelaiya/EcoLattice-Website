About

A low seater designed as a continuous, folded form that grounds the body close to the floor, seat and backrest emerging from a single gesture. It is held in place by two arched base loops that distribute weight naturally.

Its proximity to the floor makes it particularly suited to spaces that call for stillness and inward focus, adapting easily to meditation corners, reading alcoves, and informal floor-level seating in both indoor and garden settings.

Material & Finishes

Designed and produced by Ecolattice, Orbit is made from a recycled PETG glass-fibre composite. The durable, weather-resistant material makes it suitable for outdoor use while demonstrating the potential of recycled composites in large-format 3D printing.
