Wellness

EcoLattice Wellness offers customised solutions for personal comfort. These include wheelchair cushions, lumbar supports, and seating designed for long-duration use in office chairs. Each piece is made to fit the individual and aims to provide the right balance of comfort and support.

Advantages

Using pressure mapping, EcoLattice cushions are customised and made-to-measure for each user, ensuring the right level of comfort and support. Their breathable lattice structure improves airflow, while easy-to-clean, hygienic materials make them ideal for infection control and long-term use.
