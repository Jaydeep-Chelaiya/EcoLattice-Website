Prototyping Services

Using advanced manufacturing, we can produce precise foam equivalents early in the development process, testing comfort and density long before final production. At EcoLattice, we have taken prototyping further. From 20A to 80A shore hardness, we work across the full range, giving clients greater control, fewer surprises, and a more considered path from concept to product. Toyota, Haworth, and the NHS have all explored this process.

CMF Services

Khazana is EcoLattice's CMF solution service, built to bring transparency to materials and finishes across fit-outs and product development. We assess environmental impact, map supply chains, and review finish specifications to ensure every material choice meets ESG reporting standards. For designers, our advisory work offers practical tools and clear pathways toward circular economy thinking, making considered design achievable rather than aspirational. Our work spans institutions, manufacturers, and design schools, with projects including Godrej, CEPT, and NIFT.
